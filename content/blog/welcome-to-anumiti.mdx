## Why did we build Anumiti?

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) is now law. By May 2027, every business processing personal data must be compliant — or face fines up to ₹250 crore per breach. At the same time, 63 million Indian businesses process documents in 22 languages daily, often without proper extraction or verification.

We built Anumiti to solve both problems at once.

## What is KAVACH?

KAVACH (कवच — "The Shield") is our DPDP compliance platform. It handles consent management in all 22 Indian languages, WhatsApp consent flows for MSMEs, AI model governance, and automated compliance reporting. Starting at ₹2,999/month — a fraction of what enterprise tools charge.

## What is NETRA?

NETRA (नेत्र — "The Eye") is our document intelligence API. One API call extracts structured data from any Indian document — GST invoices, PAN cards, bank statements, contracts, court orders — in 22 languages with 94% accuracy. Sub-100ms processing. Developer-first.

## What makes Anumiti different?

1. **India-first**: Built around Indian documents, Indian languages, and Indian regulations — not adapted from Western tools
2. **Affordable**: Starting free, scaling from ₹2,999/month — designed for India's 63 million MSMEs
3. **Combined platform**: Compliance + intelligence in one place — because every document contains personal data
4. **Consent Manager path**: KAVACH is designed to qualify for India's Consent Manager registration (November 2026)

## What is next for Anumiti?

We're building the essential AI infrastructure layer that India Stack was missing. Over the coming months, we'll be shipping:

- DPDP compliance guides and free tools
- Open-source SDKs for Node.js and Python
- HSN code lookup and GSTIN verification tools
- Multi-language consent notice templates

Follow our journey on the blog. We'll share everything we learn about building compliance and document intelligence infrastructure for India.
